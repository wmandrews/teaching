Pick a story idea and make a quick wireframe using HTML or sketching out on a sheet of paper. The wireframe can simply be boxes with text in them like "map" or "gallery". Also, think about what tools you'll need to pull off your design and development.

Publish to your Terp Connect drive when you're done.

Story idea

Slave labor used to create common goods

Your team has done extensive reasearch and investigation into the use of slave and exploited labor in countries around the world. This work has allowed you to accurately estimate the amount of slave labor in hundreds of popular consumer products.

Your content

  • Slave labor estimates or "scores" for products used in day-to-day life, from cars to toothpaste, clothing to food.
  • Stats on countries and industries that are the worst for slave labor.
  • Hundreds of photos of forced labor factories and workers.
  • Your budget didn't allow for audio or video, so you must make the story come to life and become personal in other ways.
